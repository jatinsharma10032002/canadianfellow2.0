Post Landing Services

Temporary Accomodation Arrangements

  • Info: We understand the importance of having a comfortable and secure place to stay upon arrival in Canada. Our team assists you in finding temporary accommodations that suit your preferences, budget, and duration of stay.

  • Requirements: You'll need valid identification (passport, visa), sufficient financial resources to cover initial rental fees and deposits.

  • Procedure: We guide you through the process of researching available options, comparing prices, and booking accommodations in advance. We'll also advise you on the necessary documentation and payment details required for the booking.

Bank Account Setup Assistance

  • Info: Navigating the Canadian banking system can be challenging for newcomers. We provide step-by-step guidance on opening a bank account, which is crucial for managing your finances and receiving payments.

  • Requirements: You'll need valid identification (passport, visa) and proof of address (such as a rental agreement or utility bill .

  • Procedure: Our experts help you choose a bank that aligns with your needs, gather the necessary identification and address documents, assist you in completing the required forms, and guide you through the account activation process.

Utilities and Mobile Phone Plan Setup

  • Info: Setting up essential utilities like electricity, water, and internet, as well as acquiring a suitable mobile phone plan, are vital steps in your new life. We offer guidance to streamline these processes .

  • Requirements: You'll need proof of residence (such as a rental agreement) and identification .

  • Procedure: We help you contact utility providers, choose appropriate plans, schedule installations, and ensure you have all the necessary documentation for a smooth setup process.

SIN Application Support

  • Info: The Social Insurance Number (SIN) is essential for legal work in Canada. We provide detailed information on its significance and guide you through the application process.

  • Requirements: You'll need a valid work or study permit and identification.

  • Procedure: We assist you in applying online or at a local Service Canada Centre. We help you gather and submit the necessary documents, and once approved, you'll receive your SIN.

Healthcare Registeration Guidance

  • Info: Access to healthcare is crucial in a new country. We offer insights into the Canadian healthcare system and help you register for provincial health coverage.

  • Requirements: You'll need to apply for a provincial health card as per your province's requirements and provide identification

  • Procedure: We guide you through the health card application process, including submission and any necessary appointments. Once approved, you'll receive your health card.
